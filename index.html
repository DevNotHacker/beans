The Beanery


The Beanery is the world's largest producer of beans, feeding over 3 billion people per day. In the Classified Beanery Warehouse, we store a variety of beans. To buy a bean, go to the Shop. You can either pay with real money or Bean money, which can be earned through the Earn system.
